How the Internet Works

The internet is essentially a wire, this wire connects computers and servers, the hard drive on these machines is where data(websites) is stored. Each machine has a unique IP Address. Everyday computers/laptops/mobile devices you and I use are called clients. These devices are indirectly connected to the internet through ISP providers. When you're browsing the internet you're requesting files from servers. The servers sends the requested files back in small pieces called packets. Once the information reaches your computer the packets are reassembled back into it's original form of an email, pic, or tweet.
